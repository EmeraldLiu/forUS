About

Welcome to forUS, a site designed to help teach high school students about the wonders of U.S. History. We understand how dry history can be sometimes with teachers droning on and on about policies and what a bunch of old white men did to make our country. Our job is simple. We want to help you understand and remember the basics of U.S. History and any other important factors that come along. We hope forUS will help you enjoy history and learn history better than before.
