About this Quiz

This quiz checks your understanding of the first two periods of U.S. history, from 1491 to 1754. It begins with the Columbian Exchange and goes all the way up to the start of the French and Indian War. Important concepts include the Columbian Exchange between the Old World and the New World, various European settlements, and characteristics of English colonies. Good Luck!
